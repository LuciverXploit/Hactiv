Passphrase Requirements

Our new policy is to encourage passphrases, that is whole sentences in lieu of garbled and obfuscated short words. It is for this reason that passphrases must be at least 15 characters. While this sounds absurdly complex, consider that the sentence, " A short sentence. ", is 18 characters. (Please do not use our example passphrase as your own. That's just unwise.) You are far more likely to remember that short sentence than you are a garbled mess of letters and numbers. You can use anything from a very short sentence to a full tweet, but remember it is case-sensitive!
